Avocado Toast

A simple yet delicious breakfast featuring creamy avocado on toasted artisan bread with customizable toppings.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 5 mins
Servings: 2
Difficulty: Easy
Vegetarian Quick Meal Breakfast Healthy

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden and crisp.
  2. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper to the avocado. Mash with a fork to your desired consistency.
  4. Spread the avocado mixture evenly on the toasted bread.
  5. If adding eggs, fry or poach them while the bread toasts and place on top.
  6. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes if desired and garnish with microgreens.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
